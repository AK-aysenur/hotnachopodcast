Who Are We?

Welcome to Hot Nacho Crime, your go-to podcast for diving deep into the most chilling and mysterious true crime stories from around the world. Each episode, we uncover shocking cases, explore the dark minds behind the crimes, and piece together the details that will leave you on the edge of your seat. Whether you're a seasoned true crime enthusiast or new to the genre, we promise to deliver gripping tales that will keep you coming back for more. Join us as we explore the world of real-life mysteries, murder, and intrigue.

Nur

Hi everybody! I'm Nur and I'm currently studying criminology at University of Amsterdam. I as a person like to know everything and by that I mean 'eager to learn' everything. While I was studying I worked on a lot of cases and got the idea to start this podcast with my friend from another career background. So I hope you can enjoy our podcast!

Nive
